Design Philosophy

Warm blacks feel inhabited. Cold blacks feel industrial. We're building a cabin, not a server room.

The Glow Effect

The magic of this theme comes from layered box-shadow properties that create a soft, luminous halo around accent elements. Instead of harsh borders, we use semi-transparent coral shadows that bleed into the surrounding darkness.
