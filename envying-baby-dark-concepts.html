Gentle Text Contrast

Pure white (#ffffff) on dark backgrounds creates harsh contrast that strains eyes over long sessions. Using cream-tinted off-whites reduces fatigue while maintaining readability.

This is pure white text on a dark background. After 14 hours of debugging, your eyes will feel like they've been staring at the sun.
